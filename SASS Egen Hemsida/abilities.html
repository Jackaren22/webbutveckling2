The Stars deal 12 − 120 (based on level) (+ 5 / 10 / 15 / 20 / 25 (based on Celestial Expansion.png Celestial Expansion's Rank) (+ 25% AP) magic damage to enemies that come in contact with them as well as execute enemy Minion icon.png minions that have less than Health icon.png 25 health, including if they are brought to or below that threshold.

Upon traveling beyond the Outer Limit, the core detonates, dealing magic damage to all enemies caught within and stunning them for a duration, increased by 0% − 300% (based on time traveled).

RECAST: Aurelion Sol detonates the core early. If Aurelion Sol is faster than the missile, the missile speed will increase to keep up.

After 3 seconds or immediately after casting Comet of Legend Comet of Legend, the stars retract and Aurelion Sol gains 40% bonus movement speed, decaying over 1.5 seconds.

RECAST: Aurelion Sol commands the stars to retract early.

ACTIVE: Aurelion Sol pulls in his Stars Stars and takes flight, dashing in the target direction. While in flight, Aurelion Sol has unobstructed vision and sets his movement speed to a static amount. Comet of Legend can be recast after 1.5 seconds.

Aurelion Sol will be knocked down by any immobilizing crowd control during Comet of Legend.

If Aurelion Sol takes damage from enemy champions or turrets while in flight, Comet of Legend ends prematurely. If it is taken while Comet of Legend is not active, the ability is placed on a 4 second static cooldown.

RECAST: Aurelion Sol ends Comet of Legend early.

Aurelion Sol will be revealed while he is inside terrain and is in the line of sight of nearby enemies. The spot at which the flight will reach maximum range is highlighted to Aurelion Sol during Comet of Legend.
